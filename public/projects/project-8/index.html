Resiliency Evaluation Framework for Radial Distribution Grids

May 1, 2018 · 1 min

This project develops a general resilience evaluation framework for radial distribution feeders exposed to extreme events.

▣ Problem#

Distribution networks experience structural vulnerabilities during storms and high-impact low-probability events.

▣ Approach#

  • Model resilience through topology, damage states, and restoration pathways
  • Use graph metrics to quantify accessibility and load-serving potential
