▣ Professional Summary

I am an electrical engineer specializing in power systems, machine learning, and networked-system analytics. My work brings together physics-based modeling and modern data methods to improve grid monitoring, stability assessment, market behavior analysis, and infrastructure resilience.

My technical strengths span:

  • PMU-based event identification and oscillation analysis
  • Machine learning for forecasting, feature extraction, and continual learning
  • Electricity-market simulation and analytics (SCUC/SCED, congestion, price formation)
  • Microgrid and distribution-system resilience assessment
  • Multi-layer network and graph formulations for complex infrastructures
